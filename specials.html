Full House Package | Weekend Special | Honeymoon Package |

Full House Package

This Package is R20 000 per night for the entire Guest Villa.
Group Booking, with a maximum occupancy of 25 guests. This package requires a minimum stay of 2 nights.
Beds available: 11 Double beds and 3 single beds. Perfect for wedding parties, corporate functions and a family get together.
Honeymoon suite available.

Weekend Special

R990 per room per single person and R1300 for a double room (breakfast included). Our weekend specials are applicable during our "off season", March to September, excluding Easter and The Cape Argus Pick n Pay Cycle Tour weekends. Direct bookings only.

Honeymoon Package

The Honeymoon Package is R2300 per night for 2, this includes the Luxury Accommodation, breakfast, and a Honeymoon Bath. The Honeymoon bath includes a bottle of complimentary champagne and candle lit bubble bath with rose petals.

Time needs to be scheduled for the bath on the date of arrival for us to set this up for you.
