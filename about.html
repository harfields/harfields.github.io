Since its inception in August 1995 Harfield Guest Villa has expanded and developed into an Award Winning Guest House, being awarded First Place at the 1999/2000 Annual AA/South African Airways Accommodation Awards. View our awards/credits.

Harfield Guest Villa is situated in the heart of Harfield Village, a quaint village rich in character and steeped in indigenous history. Harfield Guest Villa is ideally located in the southern suburbs and is a 15 minutes drive from Cape Town International Airport, 5 minutes to Kirstenbosch Gardens, Newlands Cricket and Rugby Stadiums. Harfield Guest Villa is a comfortable walk to the popular shopping centre, Cavendish square and a short drive to and from Table Mountain and our famous beaches.

Yes, the Guest Villa has a shared swimming pool, situated at the back of the Guest Villa. The pool has a beautiful deck with pool loungers and an umbrella for shade. Here Guests can relax and sunbathe, while enjoying a drink served by our professional staff on duty.
